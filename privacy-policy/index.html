Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Uncle Drum is developed by Funky Frog Labs.

This Privacy Policy explains how Uncle Drum handles user information.

1. Information Stored by the App

Uncle Drum may store rhythm patterns, preferences, settings, instrument volumes and other app-related data locally on the user's device in order to provide its core functionality.

Uncle Drum does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number or precise location.

2. Advertising and Google Mobile Ads SDK

The current testing version of Uncle Drum does not display production ads.

Uncle Drum may include Google Mobile Ads SDK components for testing or future advertising features. During testing, the app may use Google-provided test ad units.

Google Mobile Ads SDK may process device-related information, including advertising identifiers or other technical data, according to Google's own policies and SDK behavior.

If production advertising is enabled in a future version, this Privacy Policy and the Google Play Data Safety section will be updated accordingly.

3. How Information Is Used

Locally stored data is used to provide the app's core features, including saving rhythm patterns, preferences, settings and app configuration.

4. Data Sharing

Uncle Drum does not sell personal data.

In the current testing version, Uncle Drum does not share personal information directly with third parties, except for data that may be processed by third-party SDKs included in the app, such as Google Mobile Ads SDK.

5. Data Deletion

Because Uncle Drum does not currently use user accounts, locally stored app data can be removed by deleting the app or clearing the app's storage from the device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Uncle Drum is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app-related information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Future Features

Future versions of Uncle Drum may include additional features such as cloud sync, user accounts, analytics, crash reporting, in-app purchases, production advertising or social features. If these features are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
